About

I am an undergraduate student in Telecommunication Engineering and Management at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. My academic interests center on artificial intelligence, multimodal interaction, and intelligent systems that connect perception algorithms with practical hardware. I am particularly interested in building reliable, human-centered AI systems for real-world environments.

Intelligent Spectrometer Guidance System

Explored an intelligent experimental guidance system combining computer-vision-based green cross recognition with sensor fusion. The project focused on using visual and sensor information to support step-by-step experimental guidance.
